# Introduction to the Physical AI Book

Welcome to the Physical AI Book! This book is designed to be a hands-on guide for beginners and intermediate learners who want to explore the exciting world of Physical AI.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI is the field of artificial intelligence that deals with systems that can perceive and interact with the physical world. This includes everything from self-driving cars and drones to smart home devices and robotic arms.

## What You Will Learn

In this book, you will learn the fundamental concepts of Physical AI, including:

-   The Sense-Think-Act cycle
-   How to use sensors to perceive the environment
-   How to use actuators to interact with the environment
-   How to build intelligent systems that can make decisions and take actions in the real world

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is for anyone who is interested in learning about Physical AI, regardless of their prior experience. If you are a student, a hobbyist, or a professional who wants to learn more about this exciting field, then this book is for you.
